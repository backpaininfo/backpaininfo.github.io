Hot/ Ice Packs

Applying hot packs help with the inflamation in the back do that a few times a day as needed when you are feeling pain.

ibuprofen

This over the counter medication has anti inflamitory properties and will help with lowering pain when used as high dose, do make sure to check with your doctor before starting any medication.

tylenol

This is a good option for those who have alergies to other medications and is a great medication to help with back pain.
