About the Spine

For people with spinal issues it is really important to care for the spine and not fully depend on the spine to do the work for us. It is important to have good posture, I have had bad posture all my life and I've developed spinal issues because of the lack of self care. I soon realized that it is vital to care for our bodies and to make sure everything we do revolves around good posture and spinal care. Our spine is really important it is after all our back backbone! We can do little changes in our lives to live a healthier more productive and pain free life with some self care and effort we can achieve that.

Who is this for?

This is for people who have spinal issues like slipped disks and other back problems, I can't guarantee that every exercise will work for you but it's all about trial and error, we have to try and test things out before we know that it won't work for us. If you are tired of going to a physical therapist and simply don't have time to drive there and pay for it you can follow simple steps to improve your health and enjoy life pain free, or less pain. I am a huge advocate for pushing people to do it even if they don't see progress right away because I believe that anyone can do these work outs from home or even from work, they are simple and easy to follow.



Relief if Sitting is an Issue

If you have problems sitting down, then here are a few steps you can take to relieve back problems when sitting down. Number one use a towel rolled up and put it behind your back to allow a nice back extension to avoid having your back curved in a way that is uncomfortable and painful on the back. Number to put an ice pack behind your back if you're sitting on the couch watching TV or doing homework this will help with the inflammation and will also help reduce the pain level slightly to make it more manageable. Number three when sitting make sure to have a good posture and feet straight not pigeon toed this will help with how your body is positioned and reduce pain this is due to being a natural form and in the right position so there's no strain on the spine.

Impacts on your stress levels

When there's stuff going on your in your life and you have pain it will make your stress levels even higher. It is really important to seek help before things get worse, stress can have a huge impact on Work school being a parent being a partner and even being the best you. We have to recognize when our stress levels are rising, stress can have a huge impact on our lives and pain just contributes to it. The best way to reduce stress is to do something about it. And the first step to do something about it is to realize that our spine will not heal on its own and that it is really important that we take action to reduce our pain instead of relying on pain medication and for the pain to go Away on its own. Our lives can get really complicated when we have pain and don't know how to manage it so the best thing we could do is educate ourselves on how to reduce pain.
